# Decentralized Prescription Drug Tracking

A blockchain-based platform for secure, transparent, and compliant tracking of prescription medications from prescriber to patient.

## Overview

This decentralized system revolutionizes prescription drug management by creating an immutable record of the entire medication lifecycle. Built on blockchain technology, our platform enhances patient safety, prevents prescription fraud, reduces medication errors, and helps combat drug diversion while maintaining strict privacy controls and regulatory compliance.

## Core Components

### Prescriber Verification Contract
- Validates the credentials and authorization status of healthcare providers
- Maintains up-to-date records of prescribing privileges and specialties
- Integrates with medical licensing boards for real-time verification
- Implements multi-factor authentication for prescribing activities
- Creates audit trails of all prescriber activities within the system

### Prescription Issuance Contract
- Records legitimate medication orders with cryptographic signatures
- Implements built-in checks against contraindications and drug interactions
- Enables secure electronic transmission of prescription details
- Prevents prescription tampering through immutable record-keeping
- Supports controlled substance prescribing with enhanced security protocols

### Pharmacy Fulfillment Contract
- Tracks the dispensing of medications with timestamp verification
- Validates prescriptions against the original issuance records
- Records lot numbers and expiration dates of dispensed medications
- Manages partial fills and refill authorizations
- Creates verifiable records of pharmacist review and approval

### Patient Verification Contract
- Confirms the identity of prescription recipients using privacy-preserving methods
- Maintains secure patient medication histories with consent-based access
- Implements sophisticated measures to prevent prescription shopping
- Enables patients to authorize medication pickup by designated individuals
- Provides patients with secure access to their complete medication records

## Benefits

- **Enhanced Safety**: Reduces medication errors through verification at each step
- **Fraud Prevention**: Makes prescription forgery and alteration virtually impossible
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Built to satisfy DEA, FDA, and HIPAA requirements
- **Supply Chain Integrity**: Helps identify counterfeit medications entering the system
- **Opioid Control**: Creates powerful tools to combat prescription drug abuse
